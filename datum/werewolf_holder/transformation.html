Proc Details

proc
transfer_organ_states
(/mob/living/carbon/transfer_from, /mob/living/carbon/transfer_to)

Transfers the state of one form's organs to those in what they're transforming into. Organs that are missing will be deleted in the new form, organs that were restored will be created in the new form, and organ damage and flags will transfer between all organs.

Arguments:

  • transfer_from - The mob organ states are being brought over from
  • transfer_to - The mob receiving the old form's organ states
